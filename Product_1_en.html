Domino entering university will take care of all your schooling worries!

Domino's is an educational promotion product for foreign children.
We will provide professional one-on-one guidance for your child, and dynamically adjust the study plan through professional assessment, as well as hire professional teachers from target schools to provide accurate teaching and interview guidance for your child, escorting your child on the way to higher education.
