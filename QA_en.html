Q:What can Domino's products for entering university do for us?

  • A:Children who come to Domino's can enroll for a full year at no cost in an educational institution program in partnership with Domino's Admissions and Progressions, and in the One-on-one tutoring by a teacher from the target school for 6 months prior to the interview. However, the number of places is limited.

    • Q:I'd like to buy home a Domino's Entrance Promotion product, what are the requirements?

  • A:Firstly, your child must be a foreigner or a Hong Kong, Macau or Taiwan citizen and meet the admission requirements of the school of their choice. Secondly, you can arrange at least 4-6 hours of tutoring time per week. Lastly, there are a limited number of 50 places available for Domino's School Placement product each year.

    • Q:What should I do if my child does not get into the designated school after purchasing the Carefree or Enjoyment Plus product?

  • A:If you are not enrolled in a school specified in the Domino's entry and are eligible for benefits, parents may be able to claim for compensation with the contract and the relevant school interview certificates.

    • Q:Is there a discount if I refer another parent?

  • A:The product cost will be spent entirely on school cooperation and pre-school educational counseling, no inflated prices, so there is no discount. But according to Domino's sharing program, if you refer your friends, Domino's will give you a surprise gift.

    • Q:Does the price change if you choose one versus multiple schools? What's the difference?

  • A:The price is fixed and does not change with the number of schools you choose. According to the school you choose, we will design and arrange the curriculum and teachers of the school you want to go to, so you can choose more than one school. We will design a curriculum that takes into account the admission requirements of many schools, so that the more children learn, the more well-rounded they become.

    • Q:Can I still attend after 120 class hours?

  • A:120 hours is six months before the exam from (the earliest school where we start interviewing) and is a one-on-one session with a classroom teacher from the school of our choice. In-person tutoring. However, given the interval between interviews at some schools, Domino's will provide an additional 4 Lessons/week of professional tutoring. In addition, our partner organizations, such as Jubilee English, Langford, and Elijah English, offer basic courses that can be continued with their The teacher is also very professional and responsible.

    • Q:Can I buy your product a few months before I enter an elite school?

  • A:No, it needs to be at least six months in advance.

    • Q:Why does it take so long to learn since I just want my child into school?

  • A:Getting into a target school is only the first step, and from what we have gathered so far, there are children who get into the school of their dreams and don't do well. This can be quite a blow to a child's confidence and motivation to learn, as he or she has to fit into the school atmosphere and keep up with everyone else's studies. Therefore, Domino's suggests that you should not only take entering a prestigious school as your goal at this stage, but also take every opportunity to learn seriously.
